About Us

Founded by Zeyuan Dong, Cheng Cheng and Phoebe Bu at Ottawa, Canada in 2021. 3TrashGuitarists aims to bring guitar players with brand new, high quality guitars. We also wish to exchange music ideas with musical friends all over the world!

Attention: This is not a real guitar selling website.
